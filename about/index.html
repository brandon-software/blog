About

Welcome!

In recent years, I’ve noticed myself losing track of the various projects I’m involved in, especially as AI accelerates my pace of work.

This blog serves multiple purposes. Primarily, it helps me reflect on my work by “debriefing” and refining my development process. It also allows me to share some of my daily and weekly efforts with family, friends, and colleagues in an accessible way, avoiding excessive technical jargon. Software development is complex, and like any specialized field, it relies on precise terminology to convey ideas.

Though not a complete accounting of my development efforts, this blog provides potential employers and collaborators with insights into my skills and contributions to software development. Through this endeavor—and my passion for problem-solving—I aim to improve as a software engineer and offer a glimpse into the day-to-day experiences of my profession.
